Chief Minister

Eknath Sambhaji Shinde
Chief Minister of Maharashtra
Read More
Eknath Sambhaji Shinde is an Indian politician who is serving as the 20th and Current Chief Minister of Maharashtra since 30 June 2022. He is also serving as the Leader of Shiv Sena since February 2023 and the Leader of the House, Maharashtra Legislative Assembly since July 2022.

Ministers

Gulab Raghunath Patil
Minister for Water Supply and Sanitation.
Read More
Gulab Raghunath Patil is a politician and incumbent Water supply and Sanitation minister of the Maharashtra state. He is a Member of Legislative Assembly, the states lower house representing rural Jalgaon constituency. He is a member of Shiv Sena party. In 2019 he got elected in 14th legislative assembly.
Dadaji Dagadu Bhuse
Minister for Ports and Mining
Read More
Dadaji Dagadu Bhuse is a member of the 13th Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. He represents the Malegaon Outer Assembly Constituency. He belongs to the Shiv Sena. It is Bhuses fourth term as Member of Legislative Assembly. In December 2014, he was appointed Minister of State for Co-operation.
Sanjay Dulichand Rathod
Minister for Food and Drugs Administration.
Read More
Sanjay Dulichand Rathod is a Shiv Sena politician from Yavatmal district, Maharashtra. He is a member of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly from Digras-Darwha Assembly Constituency. Rathod belongs to the Banjara community. He won 2014 assembly election with record margin of 79,864, second highest in Maharashtra.
Sandipanrao Bhumre
Minister for Employment Guarantee Scheme and Horticulture
Read More
Sandipanrao Bhumre is an Indian politician and he is Shiv Sena leader from Aurangabad District, Marathwada. He had been elected to Vidhan Sabha for 5 terms in 1995, 1999, 2004, 2014 and 2019. He is Chairman of Renuka Devi-Sharad Sahakari Sugar Factory, At.Vihamandwa, Tq. Paithan, Dist.Aurangabad.
Uday Samant
Minister for Industries
Read More
Uday Samant is an Indian politician and leader of Shiv Sena from Maharashtra. He is Cabinet Minister of Ministry of Industries. He is current Member of Legislative Assembly from Ratnagiri. He has been elected to Vidhan Sabha for four consecutive terms in 2004, 2009, 2014 and 2019.
Tanaji Sawant
Minister for Public Health and Family Welfare
Read More
Tanaji Sawant is a businessman, Balasahebanchi Shiv Sena politician and deputy leader from Solapur district, Maharashtra. He is current Member of Legislative Assembly from Bhoom / Paranda / Washi Vidhan Sabha constituency as a member of Shiv Sena.
Abdul Sattar Abdul Nabi
Minister for Agriculture
Read More
Abdul Sattar Abdul Nabi is an Indian politician from Maharashtra. He was briefly a minister in 2014 in the Congress government in Maharashtra. In 2019 he left Congress and joined Shiv Sena. He is a three term Member of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly from the Sillod constituency.
Deepak Vasant Kesarkar
Minister for School Education and Marathi Language
Read More
Deepak Vasant Kesarkar is a member of the 14th Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. He is the Cabinet Minister for Ministry of Education and Ministry of Marathi Language. He represents the Sawantwadi Assembly Constituency. He belongs to Shivsena.
Shambhuraj Shivajirao Desai
Minister for State Excise
Read More
Shambhuraj Shivajirao Desai is an Indian politician and Shiv Sena leader from Satara district, Maharashtra. He is a member of the 14th Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. He represents the Patan Assembly Constituency. He had been elected to Vidhan Sabha for three terms in 2004, 2014 and 2019.
